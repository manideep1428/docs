# What is Alchroma?

Alchroma transforms any image into a guided oil painting experience. It breaks down your photo into:

- 🎨 A line outline you can project onto canvas
- 🎨 Progressive brushstroke maps
- 🎨 Exact color matches with recipes for real oil paint tubes

Whether you're a beginner or pro, you'll paint stunning works step by step.
