---
title: 'Troubleshooting Upload Issues'
description: 'Common issues and solutions for image uploads'
icon: 'alert-triangle'
---

If upload fails, check your internet connection.

Try reducing the image file size.

Ensure it's RGB (not CMYK or indexed color). Pages

MDX files not included in `docs.json` will not show up in the sidebar but are accessible through the search bar and by linking directly to them.
